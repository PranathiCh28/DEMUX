🌾 Farmer Schemes in India

Government Schemes (Excluding Loans)

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Description: Crop insurance scheme to protect farmers against crop failure due to natural calamities.

Benefits:

  • Insurance coverage for crops
  • Financial support in case of crop loss

PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi

Description: Direct income support scheme providing ₹6,000 per year to farmers.

Benefits:

  • ₹2,000 every 4 months directly to farmer’s bank account
  • Helps with crop inputs and household expenses

Soil Health Card Scheme

Description: Provides farmers with soil health cards containing nutrient status of their soil.

Benefits:

  • Promotes balanced use of fertilizers
  • Improves soil fertility and productivity

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

Description: Promotes organic farming through cluster-based approach.

Benefits:

  • Financial assistance for organic farming
  • Market promotion for organic produce

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY)

Description: Scheme to improve irrigation efficiency and water use.

Benefits:

  • “Per Drop More Crop” approach
  • Subsidy for drip and sprinkler irrigation

Kisan Credit Card (KCC) – Subsidy Component

Description: Although KCC has loan features, farmers also get subsidies for crop insurance and input support.

Benefits:

  • Subsidized insurance premiums
  • Support for seeds, fertilizers, and other inputs

National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA)

Description: Supports sustainable agriculture practices to face climate change.

Benefits:

  • Financial support for rainwater harvesting
  • Soil moisture management
  • Agroforestry promotion
